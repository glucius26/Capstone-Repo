Frequently Asked Questions

How long will the earbuds take to arrive?

We are not involved in the shipping process, however
we take ~1 - 2 weeks to create your custom earbuds.
From then on, its up to the shipping company when
arrive.

Why wouldn't my doctor allow my ear
scan for your product?

The reasons you would be denied include various
medical issues. Any kind of potential aural damages
are denied access to ear scans for the purpose
of our company.

What if my earbuds no longer fit my ears?

Don't worry, we won't charge you for a whole new earbud!
In the situation that the shape of your ear has grown too
much, we will only charge a discounted fee to replace it.
You will however be required to get a new scan of your ear.

What makes our earbud superior to others is it's

Our Process:

  1. Determine if or if not you would like our product
  2. If yes, schedule an appointment with your doctor to discuss your options
  3. Your doctor must confirm that our product is healthy and safe for you
  4. If you are approved, a scan of your ear is taken and sent to our organization
  5. Once your information is sent over, you may purchase the specific model and make of the earbud desired
  6. After your order is made, we will custom make your specific earbud
  7. In one to two weeks our product will be made and shipped to you!
